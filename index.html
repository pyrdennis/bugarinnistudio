Bugarrini Studio By The Sea

Introduction

Bugarrini Studio is a small house (20sm) very close to the sea which can accommodate 2 guests. Situated at Micro Nisi, a small peninsula about 30 km away from Zakynthos Airport, it offers an environment of peace and tranquility, ideal for those who need to calm and enjoy life!

This air-conditioned studio provides a private entrance and also a private exit to the sea, which takes only a few steps away. Inside the studio, you can find a double-sized bed (150cm x 195cm), a little kitchenette with a microwave, gas hobs, and a medium-sized refrigerator.

The studio is also equipped with a kettle, an espresso machine as well as plates, forks, pots, glasses and everything you would need to prepare breakfast or a romantic dinner for you and your partner!

The stonemade yard in front, covered with a wooden shelter and furnished with a table and two armchairs, awaits you for breakfast, reading or just... enjoying the view. In the sunbeds area, our wooden comfortable sunbeds are at your disposal for resting under the Zakynthian sun, while an outside shower can offer you an instant coolness.

During your stay, we provide unlimited support for anything you would like to do in Zakynthos, like car or motorbike rentals, boat trips, hiking trails, directions for all the sights of Zakynthos, tips on where to eat and how to have fun.

  • Minimum Stay: 3 nights
  • Flexible check-in and check-out
  • Small sized pets are acceptable (under extra charge)
  • 50 Mbps fiber-optic Wi-Fi
  • Strictly no smoking inside studio
